Our Technology

Our PDF to text converter uses the pyPDF2 library to extract text from your PDF files with high accuracy. Our software currently only supports PDFs with some simple text, but will not work with complex diagrams and images. We are working towards improving upon this in the future.

The language translator on the other hand uses the google translate api to provide the best possible translation of your text. Currently only supporting three languages, we will definitely add more in near future.

We understand that your time is valuable, which is why we've developed a user-friendly interface that's easy to use and navigate. Simply upload your PDF file, select the conversion options you need, and our software will do the rest.
